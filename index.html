Who We Are

ABOUT THE COMPANY

CINGS Enterprises is a Zambian business enterprise incorporated with a vision to equip individuals with the business philosophies of enterprises — teaching them how to best venture into business regardless of the type of venture.

We are a customer-centred venture that brings to fruition the needs and wants of a client according to the safety and quality of the market, in relation to climatic changes and the need for sustainability in all industries.

The company deals in financial management, tax plans, accounting book services, project research, company profiling, graphic designing, branding, and social media management.
